    About AiGRI

    AiGRI is an innovative platform developed by the University of the Philippines Tacloban that leverages drone technology and artificial intelligence to streamline agricultural insurance processes for smallholder farmers in the Philippines.

    Our solution aligns with the Philippine Development Plan 2023-2028 and supports the National Strategy for Financial Inclusion by providing:

    • Automated crop damage assessment
    • Tamper-proof digital loss certificates
    • Integration with PCIC
    • Climate resilience data for policymakers

    How AiGRI Works

    1

    Farm Registration

    Farmers register their plots with municipal offices using GPS boundary mapping

    2

    Baseline Imaging

    LGU drone operators capture pre-disaster crop health images

    3

    Damage Assessment

    Post-disaster drone images are analyzed by AI to generate precise damage reports

    4

    Automated Claims

    Digital certificates are generated for immediate submission to PCIC
