    About AiGRI

    AiGRI is an innovative platform that uses drone technology and artificial intelligence to modernize agricultural insurance for smallholder farmers in the Philippines. It automates crop damage assessment, issues tamper-proof digital loss certificates, and integrates directly with the Philippine Crop Insurance Corporation (PCIC) for faster and more transparent claims.

    Beyond streamlining insurance, AiGRI provides climate resilience data that supports evidence-based policymaking and helps communities prepare for future risks. By enabling timely payouts and reducing reliance on outdated processes, AiGRI empowers farmers to recover quickly, sustain their livelihoods, and contribute to national food security.
